About Spectral

Hi there! We're Spectral, and we care about the Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) community and individuals with learning disabilities.

Our goal is to support the community by helping special needs centers generate their own income to provide additional aid for people with autism and other learning disabilities.

We achieve that by selling their products on this site!
